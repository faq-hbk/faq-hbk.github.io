Apex Legends

Apex Legends is a free battle royale-hero shooter game developed by Respawn Entertainment and published by Electronic Arts(EA). It was released for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, and Xbox One in 2/4/2019, and for Nintendo Switch in 3/10/2021.

Gameplay

Players form into two- or three-player squads, and select from pre-designed characters with distinctive abilities, known as "Legends".There are two gameplay modes in Apex, in the first gamemode "Battle Royale", 20 three-person squads or 30 duos land on islands, search for weapons and supplies to defeat all enemy teams.In the second gamemode "Arenas" players form to three-player squad and combat with the other team by playing the classic gamemode 3v3 team deathmatch. Teams will win when their team wins 3 pionts and at least 2 points ahead. Players spawn in a "shop" that can buy weapons, supplies and abilities to win the game.

History

Development

The developer Respawn Entertainment has created Titanfall(2014) and Titanfall 2(2016), and published Apex Legends at 2019. Apex Legends was made in the same universe, so there are some weapons and legends appeared in Titanfall and Titanfall 2.

When the Respawn Entertainment was developing Titanfall 3, they found that the game PUBG was very popular. So they decided to develope a battle royale game using the Titanfall universe, and made the new free game Apex Legends. Until now, the Respawn Entertainment has released for PC games, PlayStation 4, Xbox One, and Nitendo Switch.

Credits

After the game released, Apex Legened received favorable reviews. 8 hours after the release, players reached 1 million people, 24 hours, players reached 2.5 million people, and reached 10 million people in 72 hours. By April 2021, it had approximately 100 million players.

Esports

Now the most famous Tournament is The Apex Legends Global Series(ALGS), ALGS is sponsered by EA. ALGS divided into five regions, North America, South America, EMEA, APAC North, and APAC South. Each professional teams competes for the champion and win prizes up to $270,000 USD!(Each region has different amounts of bonuses)
